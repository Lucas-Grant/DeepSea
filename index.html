Creatures of the Deep Sea

The deep sea is home to strange and fascinating creatures that live in total darkness and under immense pressure.

Some Cool Deep Sea Creatures:

  • Anglerfish – uses a glowing lure to catch prey
  • Gulper Eel – has a giant mouth and tiny body
  • Dumbo Octopus – flaps ear-like fins to swim
  • Vampire Squid – glides with webbed arms
  • Goblin Shark – has a long, flat snout
  • Barreleye Fish – has a transparent head!
